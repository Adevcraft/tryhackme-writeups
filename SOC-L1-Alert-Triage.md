## Key Concepts

### True Positive vs False Positive
- **True Positive** — Real attack, escalate to L2 immediately
- **False Positive** — Fake alarm, document and close

### Triage Steps
1. Receive alert from SIEM
2. Investigate — IP, user, time, behavior
3. Classify — TP or FP
4. Document findings clearly
5. Escalate or close

## Personal Takeaways
- Triage is a systematic process, not guessing
- Documentation is as important as investigation
- Every missed alert can be a real breach
